About Us

The Suicide Bereavement Liaison Service has been developed in the Midlands area for families who are bereaved through suicide. The Suicide Bereavement Liaison Service is available in the following counties (Laois, Offaly, Longford and Westmeath). The service is funded by the HSE National Office of Suicide Prevention and the service provider is Midlands Living Links. The service is supported and guided by key agencies and stakeholders including the HSE, an Garda Síochána and representatives from religious and other relevant community organisations.
